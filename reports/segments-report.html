Create a New Segment

To Create a Segment, follow these steps:

  1. Login to Video Cloud
  2. In the primary navigation, click Analytics
  3. In the Custom section, click Segments
  4. To create a new segment, click Create.
  5. To set segments, follow these steps:
    • a. Name: give your segment a name
    • b. Filters: filters are split into two groups: User Filters allow us to segment based on different subscription-based attributes, and Content Filters allow us to segment based on other content attributes.
