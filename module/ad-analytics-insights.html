Introduction

Ad analytics is a free-of-charge offering to enable the Brightcove platform with a transparent, meaningful, and comprehensive set of ad metrics.

Ad analytics incorporates data from the SSAI system and a client-side plugin measuring viewability and CSAI data. Data integrations will be phased, starting with SSAI VOD, followed by SSAI live/linear, and finally, client-side data. These metrics will focus on the following:

  • Troubleshooting: What ad configurations and partners are serving ads more efficiently? What are the technical errors preventing ad impressions?
  • Reporting/Impact: Tracking Ad Impressions, fill rate, and how the audience consumes the impressions. What are the impact of ad blockers and firewalls on your ad operation?
