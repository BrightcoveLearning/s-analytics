Introduction

Ad analytics is a free-of-charge offering that provides Brightcove customers with a transparent, meaningful, and comprehensive set of ad metrics. These metrics will focus on the following:

  • Troubleshooting: What ad configurations and partners are serving ads more efficiently? Where in the workflow are we loosing revenue?
  • Reporting/Impact: Tracking Ad Impressions, fill rate, and how the audience consumes the impressions. What are the impact of ad blockers and firewalls on your ad operation?

Availability

Ad Analytics is available free of charge to Brightcove customers monetizing their VODs with SSAI. You can find them on the following platforms:

  • Video Cloud Studio
  • Beacon Studio

Ad metrics are available for Video on Demand (VOD) content monetizing with SSAI.

Navigation

  1. Open Video Cloud Studio.
  2. In the primary navigation, click Analytics.
  3. In the panel navigation, click Ad analytics.

SSAI Performance

SSAI performance metrics help users track their ad performance information.

Metrics

SSAI Performance Metrics collects and reports on the following metrics:

  • SSAI request codes: Response code for the SSAI requests.
  • Ad request errors by Ad provider: Errors for the Ad requests by Ad provider.
  • Ad request errors by Ad configuration: Errors for the Ad requests by Ad configuration.
  • Tracker errors by Ad provider: Tracking of errors by Ad provider.
  • Tracker errors by Ad configuration: Tracking of errors by Ad configuration.

Dimensions

SSAI Performance Metrics collects, aggregates, and filters the following metrics:

  • Ad config: SSAI Ad Configuration identifier.
  • Ad provider: The domain of the Ad server.

SSAI Errors

SSAI errors metrics help users track their Server-side Advertising (SSAI) errors information.

Metrics

SSAI errors metrics collects and reports on the following metrics:

  • SSAI errors: Server-side ad insertion errors.

Dimensions

SSAI errors metrics collects, aggregates, and filters the following metrics:

  • Ad config: SSAI Ad Configuration identifier.
  • Ad provider: The domain of the Ad server.
  • SSAI error: SSAI errors tracker.
  • Request error: Requests errors tracker.
  • Impression error: Impression errors tracker.

Ad Provider Performance

Ad provider performance metrics help users get information about the performance of their Ad provider.

Dimensions

Ad provider performance collects, aggregates, and filters the following metrics:

  • Ad config: SSAI Ad Configuration identifier.
  • Request type: The moment the call was sent in the Ad fulfillment cycle.
  • Ad request: Ad request tracker.
  • Calls: Number of calls from Brightcove SSAI to Ad Server.
  • Impressions: Impression tracker.

Ad Config Performance

Ad config performance metrics help users get information about the performance of their Ad configuration.

Dimensions

Ad config performance metrics must be able to collect, aggregate and filter on the following metrics:

  • Ad provider: The domain of the Ad server.
  • Ad request: Ad request tracker.
  • Calls: Number of calls.
  • Impressions: Impression tracker.

Glossary of terms

Here is the glossary of terms for Ad analytics metrics:

Data availability

Data availability for these metrics is as follows:

  • The data will be retained and available for three years.
  • The data can be exported from the UI based on the filters and aggregations at an export time in CSV format.

Response time

All the data must be two hours fresh.

Metrics

Ad analytics should be able to collect and report on the following metrics:

  • Ads scheduled: Total number of scheduled ads across all the breaks.
  • Ads inserted: Number of creatives stitched into a VOD or Live Stream.
  • Ad impressions: Number of creatives that were played.
  • Ad completion: Number of creatives watched until completion.
  • Ad impression rate: Ad Impressions/Ad Scheduled.
  • Last ad view: The time of the most recent ad impression.

Dimensions

Ad Analytics collects, aggregates, and filters the following metrics:

  • Account: Account level metrics showing the total across all dimensions.
  • Video: Aggregation by video ID.
  • Ad config: Ad config: SSAI Ad Configuration identifier.
  • Ad provider: The domain of the ad server.
