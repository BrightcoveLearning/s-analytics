Introduction

Ad insights help you understand how many viewers watch ads with your content and whether these are engaged or new viewers.

Ad insights will show the relationships between ad frequency/intensity and session length/frequency to help customers understand the most effective ad loads.

Ad insights are integrated with Brightcove Server-Side Ad Insertion (SSAI), video delivery, and analytics. These insights include:

  • Ad Tolerance:
    • Help you reduce ad abandonment based on their audience's behavior
    • Find the optimal number of ad breaks and number/length of ad pods for different playback conditions
  • Revenue Optimization:
    • Produce the best CPM rate based on ad configuration, time of the day, ad partner and tagging

For a list of ad insight terms and definitions, see the Glossary section.

Setup

Ad insights are available to Media Video Cloud customers at no extra charge. Contact your Customer Success Manager to enable your account.

Navigation

Once your account is enabled for QoE Analytics, you will find them in the Analytics module in Studio.

  1. Open Video Cloud Studio.
  2. In the primary navigation, click Analytics.
  3. In the panel navigation, click Ad Insights.

Time range

Use time range to filter the data displayed in the charts below.

Ad Consumption

Ad consumption helps you understand how your audience is engaged with advertisements over time.

Dimensions

Ad consumption by:

  • ALL - Filter by all the parameters
  • Video duration - Duration in minutes of the video (< 5, 5-10, 10-30, 30+)
  • Video type - Type of video
  • Genre - Genre watched
  • Device - Device type

Measure dimensions

  • Hours - Hours watched
  • Ad pods - Ad breaks with one or more ads shown in a sequence

Data view

  • Ads - Ad minutes watched
  • New user viewing - New engaged users watching
  • Engaged viewing - Content minutes watched
  • Average Session Duration - Average duration of each session

Key metrics

Key values to help track advertising affectiveness.

  • Ad sensitivity - Shows the impact on completion rate between no ads and having ads
